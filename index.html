Art Direction (Brand Components)

What does the company do, or offer?

Ninteneleven is an in-house video game development company that specializes in developing and selling original video games. Their primary focus is on creating immersive and entertaining gaming experiences for players of all ages and interests. They strive to offer a diverse range of high-quality video games that cater to different genres, platforms, and gaming preferences.

Who is the company to potential customers? (as a person)

Ninteneleven presents itself as a passionate and innovative gaming enthusiast to potential customers. The company is seen as a reliable and trusted friend who understands the desires and needs of gamers. Ninteneleven aims to be approachable, friendly, and dedicated to delivering unforgettable gaming experiences. They are perceived as a company that listens to its customers, values their feedback, and actively engages with the gaming community.

Why might this company entice someone to purchase a product or service?

Ninteneleven prides itself on providing exceptional high-quality gaming experiences, such as engaging gameplay, stunning visuals and compelling storytelling. Their transparent business practices such as engaging and responding to the community is what makes their games so spectacular and personal, and has created trust between their fans.
